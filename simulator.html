MoneyMentor Finance Simulation

Learn more about and explore finance using our realistic finance simulation tool!

Our interactive simulator makes learning about money simple and real. You get to make everyday choices like saving, spending, or dealing with surprise costs all in a fun and safe space. The decisions you make affect what happens next, just like real life. It's a great way to learn how money works without the stress. Whether you’re planning a budget or just figuring out what’s smart to spend on, these tools help you understand money better. They're aimed for young adults and the more you play, the more confident you’ll feel with your money. Look out for the mobile version of the simulator coming soon!
